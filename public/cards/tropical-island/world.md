### The Dual Land Concept

In Magic, mana comes from lands. Most lands produce one color. **Dual lands** produce two colorsâ€”a revolutionary concept in 1993.

### The Original Ten

Alpha included 10 dual lands:
1. **Tundra** (White/Blue)
2. **Underground Sea** (Blue/Black)
3. **Badlands** (Black/Red)
4. **Taiga** (Red/Green)
5. **Savannah** (Green/White)
6. **Scrubland** (White/Black)
7. **Volcanic Island** (Blue/Red)
8. **Bayou** (Black/Green)
9. **Plateau** (Red/White)
10. **Tropical Island** (Green/Blue)

### Why Dual Lands Matter

They have **two basic land types** without drawbacks:
- Counts as Island AND Forest
- Fetchable by search effects
- No "enters tapped" penalty

This perfect mana fixing is unmatched by any modern land.

### Blue-Green Philosophy

Tropical Island represents the merger of:
- **Blue**: Knowledge, control, manipulation
- **Green**: Growth, nature, instinct

Together: tempo, card advantage, efficient creatures.
