### Kaiba's Blue-Eyes

In the anime/manga, Seto Kaiba is obsessed with Blue-Eyes:
- Sees it as extension of himself
- Hunted down all four copies
- Destroyed one to prevent anyone else having it
- Created Blue-Eyes-themed jets, cards, everything

### Identifying 1st Edition LOB

Look for:
1. **"1st Edition"** stamp in gold
2. **LOB-001** card number
3. **SDY-001** is Starter Deck (less valuable)
4. Card code starts with **LOB-EN001** for NA
