### Time Magic in MTG

### The Philosophy of Extra Turns

Taking an extra turn means:
- Untapping all your permanents
- Drawing another card
- Attacking again
- Acting while opponent cannot respond

In essence: **playing two games while opponent plays one.**

### Temporal Manipulation Cards

Time Walk spawned a legacy:
- **Time Warp** (5 mana) - "Fixed" version
- **Temporal Manipulation** (5 mana) - Functional reprint
- **Nexus of Fate** (7 mana) - Modern extra turn

All cost 3-5 more mana than Time Walk, showing how broken the original is.
