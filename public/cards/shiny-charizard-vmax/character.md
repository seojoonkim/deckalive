### What Makes It "Shiny"

Regular Charizard: Orange scales
**Shiny Charizard: Black/charcoal scales**

The shiny coloration is:
- Extremely rare in games (~1/4096)
- Highly coveted by collectors
- Visually striking

### VMAX Mechanics

VMAX represents Gigantamax form:
- Massive HP (300+)
- Powerful G-Max moves
- Gives 3 prize cards when KO'd
